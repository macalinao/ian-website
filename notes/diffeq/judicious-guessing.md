title: Judicious Guessing

Judicious guessing can be used to guess for a solution.

### Example 1

Solve for $y(t)$.

$$
y'' + 4y = t\sin(2t)
$$

Solving for the roots of the characteristic equation

$$
r^2 + 4 = 0
$$

we get $r = \pm 2i$, so

$$
y_h = c_1\cos(2t) + c_2\sin(2t)
$$
